## About PDF Wandler

PDF Wandler is a collection of free and easy-to-use online PDF tools designed to make you more productive. Whether you need to compress PDFs, merge multiple files, or convert images to PDF, we've got you covered.

## Our Features

- **Image to PDF**: Convert JPG and other image formats to PDF instantly
- **Merge PDF**: Combine multiple PDF files into a single document
- **Compress PDF**: Reduce file sizes without compromising quality

## Privacy First

All your data is processed on secure German servers, and we never store your files permanently. Your privacy is our top priority.
